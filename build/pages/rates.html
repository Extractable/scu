RATES & TERMS

Rates quoted are effective as of 10/24/2019 and are subject to change without notice.

SchoolsFirst FCU's loan program features a range of interest rates for some of its cnsumer loans. A rate is based on variety of factors, include the applicant's credit rating. Loan terms are based on various factors, including the amount financed.

SchoolsFirst FCU's mazimim aggregate personal loan limit is $50,000 per qualifying Member, including all individual and joint personal accounts.

All loans subject to credit approval.

For more information, visit our website at www.schoolsfirstfcu.org or call 800.462.8328.
